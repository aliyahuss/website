About HAE Fashion

ABOUT THE FOUNDERS

HAE Fashion is a British based fashion blog, created in 2019 by a group of university students. We aim to target unique fashion styles and embrace a variety of cultures which mainstream fashion blogs may not include. Modern day society allows indiviudals to express themselves through a variety of mediums and we wanted to capture this in our blog.

OUR INSPIRATION

Our inspiration comes from a wide range of sources who try to further cultural diversity and representation in the modelling community. One of the fashion designers we look up to the most, Agatha Ruiz de la Prada, entered fashion in 1981, where she opened her first studio in the Spanish capital, beginning to participate in fashion shows to promote her clothing line. She started to gain international recognition from fashion shows in Spain, France, Italy, Tokyo, and many others. Her fashion brand was licensed in 1991 and it included clothing for men women and children.
